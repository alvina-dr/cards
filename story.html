The World of the Cards

For many years, cards represented the world. Their world may be like ours. The story begins with one card. This card wanted to travel the world. It wanted to see its people and their way of life. They say travelling is growing up. And the lonely card learned so much.

Castle

Its first trip was to Asia. There, it discovered a castle fully made of cards. And it learned that each card composing it had an important role. It saw the sharing and the responsibility. For if only one card falls, all the other falls with it. Thinking back to all the other cards she was part of before getting separated, her deck, her family, she knew that she wasn't really alone, that she was a part of a whole. (Responsabilité, Société, Partage)

Meadow :

It looked at a vast meadow and in its center there was a lonely windmill solely turning because of the wind. Enough energy was made to power the little house beside it. Entering the house there was a library. In the library it looked the letter and on the side she watched the little flower. The flower was happy and when she was watering the flower grow until becoming a tree. And many card drop of the tree.

Plane :

Above the tree it looked at a plane. It jumped in the plane During the flight it watched the seventh continent. The polution, the waste . The planet is seen from above. it is easier to see the problems from above

Ship :

The plane started to go down, go down... Then after a moment the plane transformed into a ship. The capitain of the ship is an old marine. And on the water there are dead cards. On the card there was no drawing anymore. After a moment the ship bumped a new country.
