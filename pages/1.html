Its first trip was to Asia. There, it discovered a castle fully made of cards. And it learned that each card composing it had an important role. It saw the sharing and the responsibility. For if only one card falls, all the other falls with it. Thinking back to all the other cards she was part of before getting separated, her deck, her family, she knew that she wasn't really alone, that she was a part of a whole.
